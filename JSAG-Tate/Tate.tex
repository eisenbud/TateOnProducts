\begin{abstract}
We describe the  Macaulay2 package TateOnProducts and its capabilities, which include computing cohomology tables of sheaves
on products of projective spaces and the derived category pushForward of a sheaf under a morphism from a projective scheme to
a projective space.

\section*{Introduction}

In their 1978 papers Beilinson and Bernstein-Gelfand-Gelfand both gave a description of the derived category $D^b(\PP^n)$ of bounded complexes of coherent sheaves on $\PP^n$.

The heart of Beilinson argument is that both $\{ \sO(-k) | k=1,\ldots,n\}$ and 
$\{ \Lambda^k U | k=0, \ldots, n\}$ form  what is nowadays called  a semi-orthogonal full exceptional exceptional sequences for $D^b(\PP^n)$.
Here $U$ denotes the universal rank $n$ subbundle on $\PP^n$:
$$ 0 \to U \to \sO^{n+1} \to \sO(1) \to 0.$$ 
A key point is, that the diagonal $\Delta \subset \PP^n \times \PP^n$ has a Koszul resolution
$$ 
0 \to \Lambda^n U \boxtimes \sO(-n) \to \ldots \to U \boxtimes \sO(-1) \to \sO \to \sO_\Delta \to 0
$$
where $A \boxtimes B = pr_1^* A \otimes pr_2^* B$ for  coherent sheaf $A,B$ on $\PP^n$ denotes their tensor product on $\PP^n \times \PP^n$ after pull back along the first and second projection.

The heart of the argument of BGG is a pair of adjoint functors. Let $S=K[x_0,\ldots,x_n]$ denote the homogeneous coordinate ring of $\PP^n=\PP(W)$ and let $E = \Lambda V$ with $V=W^*$ denote the Koszul dual exterior algebra \cite{Koszul duality}.
The there exists a pair of functors $L$ from the category of finitely generated graded $E$-modules to the category of linear complex of $S$-modules
$$ L: grmod(E) \to linCplx(S)$$
$$ P =\oplus_i P_i \mapsto  \ldots \to P_i \otimes_K S(i) \to P_{i-1} \otimes _K S(i+1) \to \ldots $$
